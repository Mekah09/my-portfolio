    About me.

    I’m a passionate and driven aspiring frontend developer currently building my skills through hands-on projects and continuous learning. Over the past weeks, I’ve worked on HTML, CSS, Tailwind CSS, responsive design, and Git/GitHub, applying each skill directly into real-world exercises like creating navigation bars, footers, content sections, and my personal portfolio.


    I enjoy turning ideas into functional, user-friendly designs, and I’m particularly drawn to tools and workflows that make development faster and more efficient. Learning in public has been a big part of my journey, I share my progress regularly, not only to stay accountable but also to connect with others in tech.


    My goal is to grow into a versatile frontend developer, capable of building clean, responsive, and impactful web experiences while collaborating effectively with other developers. I’m open to opportunities, mentorship, and projects where I can contribute, learn, and keep improving.
